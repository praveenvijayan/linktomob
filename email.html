What is linktomob?

Link to mobile aims to easily share links across browsers and devices. Remember a single unique url and access linkmarked sites everyware.


Activate your url

{{link}}

Features

1. No browser plugins
2. No login
3. Quick and easy sharing
4. Single url & access linkmarked site across multiple devices

How to use

1. You will get a unique URL (linktomob.appspot.com/{yourid}) after activation
2. Drag and drop linkmob bookmarklet into bookmark.
3. Browse to any site and click on linktomob bookmarklet.
4. Your unique URL now has the bookmarked link.
5. Using your unique link (http://linktomob.appspot.com/{yourid}) you can browse the site on any device.

Other notes

1. List all linkmarked links using http://linktomob.appspot.com/{yourid}:no (Eg: linktomob.appspot.com/1:10 - will list last 10 entries)
2. Regenerate bookmarklet : http://linktomob.appspot.com/{yourid}/bookmark
